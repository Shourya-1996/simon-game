Rules

  1. Press the "Start" button or the press any key on the keyboard to initiate the game.
  2. The color tile will be flashed and play a sound to start the sequence.
  3. Pay attention to the sequence of colors and sounds that the tiles displays and plays.
  4. After the game shows the first color, repeat the same color by pressing the corresponding color tile.
  5. The game will progress in difficulty as the sequence gets longer with each successful turn.
  6. Try to remember and repeat the entire sequence correctly to advance in the game.
  7. If a player makes a mistake by pressing the wrong button or pressing a button out of sequence, the game will end for that player.
  8. The player who can successfully repeat the longest sequence without making a mistake is the winner.
